    About us

    The company is 100% black owned private company (Pty Ltd). The company is led by a young, motivated and skilled individual with a passion to succeed. Throughout the last 7 years, our passion for transport has crystallized into forming this company. We also specialize in Transport, Septic Tank services, Construction, Tarpaulin and General Supplies. The team running these operations is highly focused and energized.

    We strive to deliver a best customer experience. We promise to deliver goods and passengers, with a great sense of urgency. Being a small firm, we have the time and attention units to exceed the expectations of our customers. Being part of a trusted transport network, we ensure the immediate availability of our fleet. Our fleet is fitted with satellite tracking, to pinpoint your cargo to the exact location. We aim to transport cargo/passengers throughout South Africa and its nearest borders.

    DANNY AND THE AUSTIN (PTY) LTD is a registered entity and has commenced operations since 27/01/2017. We provide attractive, eco-friendly and customised solutions to suit the client needs. We provide a wide variety of services and products that are guaranteed to meet and exceed any expectation and/or standard. We provide not only guarantee and durability but great quality, excellent service and innovation. We deliver optimum quality service. There is no job too BIG or too small for us
